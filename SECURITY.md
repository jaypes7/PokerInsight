# Security Policy

## Reporting a Vulnerability

Please report suspected vulnerabilities to security@pokerinsight.app.

Do not include secrets, real hand histories, production data, or exploit details
in public issues. We aim to acknowledge reports within five business days.

## Scope

In scope:
- Authentication and authorization flaws.
- Tenant isolation bypasses.
- Secret exposure.
- Upload or parser vulnerabilities.
- Billing webhook integrity issues.

Out of scope:
- Social engineering.
- Denial of service without a concrete mitigation proposal.
- Findings requiring access to data you do not own.
